Program

The scope of the workshop is to review developments, current status, interfaces and technological impact of particle physics, astrophysics and cosmology in both theoretical and experimental aspects. Expected topics include:

  • Standard model of elementary particles and beyond,
  • General relativity and modified theories of gravity,
  • Cosmological models,
  • Neutrino physics, CP violation, etc., in particle physics and in cosmology,
  • Gravitational waves and testing gravitational theories,
  • Dark matter, dark energies, etc. , in particle physics and in cosmology,
  • Structure and dynamics of the Universe: inflation, dark matter, dark energy, black holes, holography, galaxies, etc.,
  • Phenomenology at current and future colliders
  • Physics and technology
The full program is expected to be announced on September, 2025.
